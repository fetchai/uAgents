About UsThis list is a collation of the work we do at TheMindClan.com.

We are a free mental health platform dedicated to giving individuals access to a curated list of resources for inclusive and intersectional mental health care. These include therapists, support groups, workshops, blog articles, audios/videos, and other resources.

We hope to make mental health a journey that centers your choice, your identity, your context and mobilizes community support.s
